GDPR



Consent to the processing of personal data


Scope of processing your personal data
The administrator will process your personal data to the extent that you have provided them in connection with the administrator's marketing activities, specifically with registration on the website www.soireespecialistheaven.sk in the form of full registration or abbreviated registration when creating non-binding reservation. The administrator will primarily process the name, surname, e-mail, telephone number and residential address or address of the contact point.


Purpose of processing your personal data
The Administrator will process your personal data for the purposes of correct and trouble-free implementation of the services ordered by you. Data processing will only be associated with the order you created and its smooth implementation.


Legal basis for processing personal data
The legal basis for processing is your consent and the fact that your personal data are necessary for the correct and trouble-free implementation of the services you ordered. Personal data is processed in accordance with the General Regulation on the Protection of Personal Data - Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 - (hereinafter referred to as "GDPR").


Period after which your personal data will be processed
The administrator will process your personal data for a period of 2 years from your registration on the website www.soireespecialistheaven.sk or from your order for the service on the website www.soireespecialistheaven.sk, if this you do not withdraw consent.


Revocation of consent to the processing of personal data
You can revoke your voluntarily granted consent to the processing of personal data at any time, free of charge, by sending an email to the address: info@soireespecialistheaven.sk. Revocation of consent does not affect the legality of processing based on consent that was given before its revocation. Revocation of consent also does not affect the processing of personal data that the administrator processes on the basis of a different legal basis than consent.


Persons with access to your personal data
Your personal data will be accessed by the administrator, his employees and possibly third parties directly connected only with the correct and problem-free execution of your order and who provide appropriate guarantees and whose processing meets all requirements GDPR, and which will sufficiently protect your rights and your personal data. These persons are mainly suppliers of ordered services. However, these persons will have access to this data only for a period of time that is absolutely necessary and to the extent that it is absolutely necessary (name, surname and telephone number), if it is necessary for the provision of the service and the fulfillment of legal requirements.


Contact details of the administrator
You can contact the administrator via email: info@soireespecialistheaven.sk, or in writing at the address of the administrator's business. The administrator is entitled to demand proof of your identity in order to prevent unauthorized access to your personal data. In order to improve the quality of services and keep records of the administrator's fulfillment of legal obligations, all communication between you and the administrator can be monitored.


Your rights related to the protection of your personal data
You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time, correct or supplement personal data, request restriction of processing, object to the processing of personal data, request data transfer, access to your personal data, be informed on breach of personal data security, erasure and other rights set forth in the GDPR.


Right to object
You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data if the administrator processes them for direct marketing purposes. The objection must be submitted to the administrator. In the event that you raise an objection to processing for the purpose of direct marketing, the administrator will not further process personal data to this extent, if he does not demonstrate serious legitimate reasons for processing that outweigh your interests or rights and freedoms or for the determination, exercise or defense of legal claims. More detailed information about this right is mainly contained in Art. 21 GDPR.


The right to file a complaint with the supervisory authority
At any time, you can file a complaint with the supervisory authority regarding the processing of your personal data or non-fulfillment of the administrator's obligations arising from the GDPR. The supervisory authority in the Slovak Republic is the Personal Data Protection Office, with headquarters in Bratislava, Hraničná 12, 82007 - https://dataprotection.gov.sk/uoou/


Obligation to provide personal data. Consequences of not providing personal data
You provide your personal data quite voluntarily. You are under no obligation to provide them. If you do not provide your personal data, you will not face any penalty. However, if you do not provide personal data to the administrator, he will not be able to implement the service you ordered smoothly and correctly.
