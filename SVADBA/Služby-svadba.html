Kollárovci

The band Kollárovci is an original group of young, talented musicians. With their musical art, they have been entertaining and cheering up listeners at home and beyond the borders of Slovakia for many years. Their live music, spirited singing and humorous words create an unforgettable atmosphere that will bring a smile, enthusiasm and good mood to your face. They have been successfully operating on the market since 1997 and with their goral temperament they have won the favor of many fans.

The backbone of their repertoire is the Slovak folk song, which thanks to the Kollárovs gets a new look, pleasant even for those who have never searched for this genre. In their presentation, you can also listen to your own compositions from their author's workshop. Kollarovci have released 11 music CDs and 4 DVDs, which should not be missing in your home. They perform many concerts and performances every year. The band consists of six members, three of whom are the Kollárov brothers. The cast of instruments is also varied: violin, double bass, soprano and alto saxophone, dulcimer, accordion, percussion instruments.
